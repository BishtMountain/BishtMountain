Meet Hemant Singh Bisht

Adventure Rider • Expedition Leader • Explorer

Hi, I'm Hemant Singh Bisht.

My adventure began with my first ride to Ladakh in 2015. What started as a single journey soon became a lifelong passion for exploring the Himalayas.

Since then, I have organized 100+ motorcycle expeditions across India, with a special focus on Leh-Ladakh, Spiti Valley, and remote offbeat Himalayan destinations.

Rather than following crowded tourist routes, I create experiences that take riders to hidden valleys, high mountain passes, remote villages, and breathtaking landscapes that few people ever get to explore.

Whether it's the legendary roads of Leh-Ladakh, the rugged beauty of Spiti, or unexplored Himalayan trails, every expedition is planned with one goal—to deliver a safe, unforgettable, and truly unique adventure.

Every ride is more than a destination. It's a story waiting to be lived.
