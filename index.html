About Hemant Singh Bisht

My journey began with my first motorcycle expedition to Ladakh in 2015. Since then I have organized 100+ motorcycle expeditions across Leh–Ladakh, Spiti, Zanskar, Upper Mustang, Umling La, Milam and many hidden Himalayan routes.

BishtMountain is about exploring beyond tourist roads—remote villages, high mountain passes and unforgettable adventures with like-minded riders.
