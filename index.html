About Hemant Singh Bisht

My journey began with my first motorcycle expedition to Ladakh in 2015. What started as a solo adventure soon became a passion for exploring the untouched Himalayas and sharing those experiences with fellow riders.

Over the years I have successfully organized more than 100 motorcycle expeditions across Leh-Ladakh, Spiti Valley, Zanskar, Umling La, Upper Mustang, Milam and many hidden Himalayan destinations.

My rides are built for riders who seek more than just famous tourist roads. Every expedition is carefully planned to explore remote villages, high mountain passes, breathtaking landscapes and unforgettable adventures.

Whether you're joining your first Himalayan ride or chasing your next big adventure, the goal remains the same— Ride Beyond Every Pass.
