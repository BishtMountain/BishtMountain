About Hemant Singh Bisht

I'm Hemant Singh Bisht, the founder of BishtMountain. My journey began with my first motorcycle expedition to Ladakh in 2015. What started as a solo adventure soon evolved into a lifelong passion for exploring remote mountain landscapes and creating unforgettable riding experiences for others.

Over the years, I've led 100+ motorcycle expeditions cross some of the most iconic and challenging Himalayan routes, ncluding Leh–Ladakh, Spiti Valley, Zanskar, Umling La, Upper Mustang, Milam, and many lesser-known mountain trails.

At BishtMountain, every expedition is designed with a focus on authentic adventure rather than just popular tourist destinations. From remote villages and high-altitude passes to breathtaking landscapes and local culture, every journey is carefully planned to deliver a safe, immersive, and memorable experience.

Whether you're preparing for your first Himalayan ride or searching for your next great adventure, my mission is simple—to help you Ride Beyond Every Pass. Ride Beyond Every Pass.
